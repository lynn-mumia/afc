BASIL FARMING

Growing Herbs

Basil is an aromatic herb from the mint family.The herb is used as a spice for cooking and also for making medicines.It is quick in maturing and takes 30 days from the day of planting to the harvest day.Basils are sensitive and need extra care.

SAUTI YA MKULIMA

Kyai Irrigation Scheme

Musembi a farmer from Kibwezi uses river water to do his farming. He uses drip irrigation which minimizes water usage. On his view, farmers and government need to find a mechanism to collect rain water which is lost during rainy season so that it can aid during dry times.



NGULU SPRINGS

Irrigation pays

Meet the Hottest Farmer.The founder of Ngulu Springs.At his farm in Kibwezi, he uses water from Athi River to do irrigation on his 6 hactares of chillies farm. Amember of AFFAC narrates how the club has helped him. Farming is all about passion and hardwork.



FARMING VS SPACE

POULTRY FARMING

African Poultry Farming

Poultry farming is among the most feared business venture due to high risks involved due to diseases. In usomi farm in kisaju,Mr Dennis has gone beyond the risk to raising over 10 thousand chicken in a free range.use of hydroponically grown barley is used to feed the chicken and with the usomi rubi app ,he links the market directly without involving brockers.
