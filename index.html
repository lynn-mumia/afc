WELCOME TO AFFAC!

A farmers community driven by the farmer. The One stop Place for farmers where stories, experiences and encouragement are shared. Farmers increase their productivity, get markets for their produce & improve their livelihoods towards zero hunger in Africa.
AFFAC helps you to identify underlying and deep down hidden reasons for your productivity in your farm. This is because even though mostly we are struck by food crisis because of poor or recent farming practises, it's the underlying reasons that matter the most.

AFFAC has a proven record of higher productivity ratio compared to any other farming support platforms in Africa. Through this platform, many farmers have attained knowledge, gained advice, received recommendation, provided with market for their produce and received free agronomic support.
