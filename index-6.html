  • Enjoy the Benefits.

Benefits

  • Market Linkage.

    The model of social enterprise that facilitates trade relationship between the farmer and the market(buyer).

  • Agronomic services.

    Agronomic support is the key factor for the long-term success in farming. This is a provision made by Africa Farmers Club.

  • Farm Trainings.

    Given that farmers are a prcatical species, Africa Farmers Club provides farmers the opportunity to walk and see for themselves what their career can achieve them.

  • Discounts on selective farm inputs.

    Farm inputs are inclusive of fertilizers, pesticides, equipments or any sort of substance used by a farmer for better production. Africa Farmers Club enables cheaper and easy access to such inputs.
